Help & User Guide

Welcome to the Ocean View Resort Reservation Management System. This guide will help new staff members use the system effectively.

01
Creating a New Reservation
Click the New Reservation tab. Complete all fields marked with an asterisk (*). Select the desired room type by clicking on it. Click Confirm Reservation — a unique OVR-XXX number will be automatically generated and displayed.
02
Viewing a Booking
Navigate to the View Booking tab. Enter the guest's reservation number in the format OVR-001. Click Search to retrieve and display the complete booking information including guest details and room information.
03
Generating a Bill
Go to the Calculate Bill tab. Enter the reservation number and click Generate Bill. The system automatically calculates the total cost based on the number of nights multiplied by the room rate, plus 15% government tax.
04
Room Rates & Types
Standard Room: LKR 15,000 per night. Deluxe Ocean Room: LKR 25,000 per night. Presidential Suite: LKR 45,000 per night. All rates are subject to 15% government taxes and service charges.
05
Validation & Rules
All fields marked * are mandatory. Check-out date must always be after the check-in date. Contact numbers must include the country code (+94). A valid NIC or passport number is required for every guest registration.
06
System Security
Always sign out after completing your session, especially on shared computers. Never share your login credentials with others. For password resets or technical issues, contact the IT department at it@oceanviewresort.lk or ext. 201.
